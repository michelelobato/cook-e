Cook-E FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

Q: What is Cook-E?

A: Cook-E is an exclusive website designed for users, focusing on enhancing their restaurant experiences. It offers features such as user-generated reviews, restaurant profiles, menus, galleries, contact information, ratings, and promotions.

Q: What can I expect from a restaurant profile?

A: Restaurant profiles are publicly editable, similar to a wiki, allowing users and owners to contribute information, upload menus, photos, contact details, and receive user-generated ratings and reviews.

Q: How can I share my experience with a restaurant?

A: Users can write detailed reviews, rate restaurants on a scale of 1 to 5 stars.

Q: How do I sign up for Cook-E?

A: Click "Sign Up" on the homepage, provide your full name, unique username, secure password, email, and phone number.

Q: How do I log in to Cook-E?

A: Click the "Log In" button, enter your unique username and password, then click "Log In" to access your Cook-E account.
